What is HCACHE in an Atmel SAM4S MCU?

up vote 1 down vote favorite

In the Atmel SAM4S datasheet, there is mention of HCACHE in the configuration summary table. But I cannot find this term elsewhere in the datasheet. What is it?

cortex-m4

It is a simple CPU cache. I'm not sure sure why they use the phrase "HCACHE" or what it stands for, but it is a normal combined instruction and data cache.
